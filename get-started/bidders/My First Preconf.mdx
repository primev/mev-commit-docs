### Pre-requisites
Make sure you:
1. Get a [mev-commit Bidder Node up and running](https://docs.primev.xyz/get-started/quickstart).
2. [Fund your node](https://docs.primev.xyz/get-started/bidders/fund-node) so you have the funds to submit bids.
